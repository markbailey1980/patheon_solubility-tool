Aqueous Solubility

  • < .001 mg/mL
  • 0.001 - 0.1 mg/mL
  • > 0.1 mg/mL

Aqueous Solubility

The maximum concentration of a drug that will dissolve in pure water at a reference temperature.

Permeability

  • < 5 x 10-6 cm/s
  • ≥ 5 x 10-6 cm/s

Permeability

The rate at which a drug passes through biological membranes. A standard reference is the Caco-2 permeability assay.

LogP

  • < 1
  • 1 - 4
  • > 4

LogP

The partition coefficient is the ratio of drug concentration in octanol versus that in water.

Melting Point

  • < 100 ℃
  • 100 – 200 ℃
  • > 200 ℃

Melting Point

The temperature at which a drug melts.
